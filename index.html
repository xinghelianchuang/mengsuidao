About Yuanju Textile

Chaoyang Yuanju Textile Co., Ltd. was established in May 2024, located in Beipiao City, Chaoyang. We specialize in coral velvet, dry hair towels, composite fabrics and more — producing towels, bath towels, dry hair towels, bathrobes, bath caps, and car wiping towels.

Our spirit is "Service, Value, Integrity." We commit to provide high quality products and competitive prices. With more than 16 years' production experience, two big spinning mills and a fabric factory with one-stop system, we are in the leading position in this industry. Products exported to Europe, America, Japan, South Korea, Southeast Asia, South Asia, Central Asia, China and Africa.
